There's always room for improvement

Today weâ€™re launching a new redesign, as we start to refresh our site in general. We loved our old look and know many felt the same. And yet, here we are explaining why we decided to evolve it.

  • Features

    • We added some features that we felt all BCIT courses should have.

    • Schedule Page

      • This page is a life saver for students, when done right. Students are able to stay on top of deadlines and never again forget to study for an upcoming quiz. It eases the burden off teachers as well, to not have to remind students when things are due or whether there will be a quiz next class.

    • Navigation Bar

      • A clean navigation bar is always a good way to go -- the cleaner it is, the easier it is to use, hence why the navigation also collapses into a hamburger menu when on mobile.

    • Search Bar

      • Every good website should have a search bar. Students can look up lectures, assignments, resources with even more ease.

  • Bootstrap

    • We used bootstrap to streamline and bootstrap our prototype to communicate the idea across in a simplistic style.

  • Navigation

    • We got rid of the dropdown menu, and decided to have all available pages shown in plain sight for better navigation.

  • For future reference...

    • Integrating grades into assignment and quiz pages directly
